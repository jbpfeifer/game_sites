This is the public home for the games I make as an indie developer.

I first put it together because app stores and ad platforms need the usual official pages, but I did not want those links to live on a dead-looking paperwork site. I wanted one place that could do both: host the games themselves and keep the practical details easy to find.

If you landed here from Google Play, a support link, or a privacy page, you are in the right place.
